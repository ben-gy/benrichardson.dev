Common Questions

How does the app use AI?

The app uses Apple Intelligence, which runs entirely on your device. All AI processing happens locally on your iPhone or iPad, so your data never leaves your device.

Is my data private?

Yes. The app doesn't collect any personal data. Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no data sharing.

Does it need internet?

No. The app works completely offline since all processing happens on your device.

Found a bug?

Please email support@benrichardson.dev with a description of what happened. If you can include your device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce it, that helps a lot.

Have a feature idea?

I'd love to hear it. Send it to support@benrichardson.dev. I can't promise to build everything, but I do read all suggestions.

How do I delete my data?

Just uninstall the app. Since everything is stored locally on your device, deleting the app removes all data.

Which devices work?

The app runs on iPhone and iPad. Check the App Store listing for specific requirements.

Quick Troubleshooting

Before reaching out, try these:

  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart the app
  • Make sure your device has enough storage

Enjoying the app?

An App Store review would be really appreciated. It helps others find the app.

If something's not working, please email me first so I can try to fix it.
