Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 16, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how this application handles your information. The short version: your data stays on your device.

Privacy at a Glance: This app does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.

Information Collection

This app does not collect any personal information. It operates entirely on your device without requiring:

  • User accounts or registration
  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics or tracking

Any data you create or input within the app remains stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

On-Device Processing

The app uses Apple's on-device machine learning (Apple Intelligence) to provide its features. This means:

  • All AI processing happens locally on your iPhone or iPad
  • Your data never leaves your device for AI processing
  • No information is sent to external AI services or cloud servers
  • Processing uses Apple's secure, privacy-focused frameworks

Data Storage

All data created within the app is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Any content you create or save
  • App preferences and settings
  • Cache and temporary files

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through iOS Settings.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data brokers. There is:

  • No data sharing with third parties
  • No advertisements
  • No tracking

The only external services used are Apple's standard frameworks, which are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Because the app does not collect any personal information, it is safe for users of all ages and complies with COPPA by design.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible only to you
  • Deletion: Remove all data by uninstalling the app
  • Portability: Your data exists only on your device under your control

For users in the EU, California, or other regions with privacy regulations: because no personal data is collected or stored, data subject rights are inherently satisfied.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Material changes will be noted in App Store update notes.
